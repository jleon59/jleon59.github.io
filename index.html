How does it work?

Groundbreaker works similarly to a stock market: the more investments ideas receive, the more they go up. Users are able to advance ideas by spending their 'lamps', the app's unique currency.



  • 1
    Pick a particular topic or problem, describe it and invite a group to ideate.
  • 2
    Team members will individually place suggestions to address the issue and invest on each other's ideas. Those who receive enough 'lamps' will then move on to phase 2.
  • 3
    A quick question-and-answer dynamic will help further develop ideas into plans. Once it is done, participants must seek extra investments so their idea win the 'Groundbreaker' status.
  • 4
    Once an idea reaches 'Groundbreaker' status, supporters get back all their invested 'lamps' plus a bonus. You are now ready to put your plan into action!
